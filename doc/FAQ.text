FAQ :
Q1) How to copy a gtktextiter?
A1) To copy [iter] use 
    [let new_iter = GtkText.Iter.copy iter] or 
    [let new_iter = GText.iter iter].
    Thereafter one can use methods with side effects of [new_iter] without
    affecting [iter]. 
    Do not use [let new_iter = new GText.iter iter]: it does not copy [iter].
